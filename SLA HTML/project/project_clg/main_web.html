About Us:

🏛️ Overview & Location

  • Established in 2001, JEC is a private, AICTE‑approved institution and affiliated with Anna University in Chennai
  • The campus sprawls over approximately 48 acres in Jeppiaar Nagar, near Old Mamallapuram Road (Rajiv Gandhi Salai), Semmenchery, Chennai

📚 Courses & Academics

  • UG programs: B.E. and B.Tech in specializations like Computer Science, IT, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, Aeronautical, Electronics & Instrumentation, Food Biotechnology, etc.
  • PG programs: M.E/M.Tech (CSE, Bio‑Technology), MBA (Finance, HR, Marketing, Operations, Systems), and MCA
  • Eligibility: Minimum 50% in Class 12 PCM for UG (TNEA); PG requires 50% in graduation with valid GATE or TANCET score

🏫 Infrastructure & Student Life

  • Facilities include air‑conditioned labs, discipline‑based and common labs, modern classrooms, seminar halls, auditoria, and a central library with over 1,04,000 volumes, 8,292 titles, ~1,273 e‑resources (ASME, IELonline, SpringerLink), and reprographic facilities
  • The campus houses hostels (AC/non‑AC) fully equipped with mess, recreation center, ATM, medical room, and more. Separate hostels are provided for boys and girls
  • A capacity of ~40 buses covers Chennai’s suburbs for day scholars; private vehicles are restricted to ensure safety and punctuality
  • Mess facilities serve vegetarian and non‑vegetarian meals, snacks, tea, ice creams, with capacity for ~2,500 students at a time

👥 Student Feedback & Perspectives

  • Verified reviews praise the college’s laboratories, infrastructure, faculty support, and campus atmosphere. One reviewer cited: “Professors were knowledgeable ... Wi‑Fi and laboratory ... hostels are very neat ...”
  • On the flip side, some negative opinions emerged on social media concerning placement transparency and campus environment. On Reddit, someone remarked:
  • “Most probably you will suffer for 4 years and finally get a TCS job with 3.5 LPA package for your troubles.”
  • t's worth treating these as anecdotal; individual experiences may vary.

  • Campus includes a medical center with on‑campus doctor and emergency services, plus an in-campus Indian Bank ATM branch

Dr.Jeppiaar :

👤 Founder: Colonel Dr. Jeppiaar (J. Pangu Raj, also JPR) :

  • Born: June 11, 1931 | Died: June 18, 2016 at age 85 in Chennai
  • Hometown: Muttom, Kanyakumari District, Tamil Nadu
  • Education: B.L. (Madras University), M.A. (Mysore University), Ph.D. in Water Resource Management (Anna University), earned in his 70s

🏛️ Career Path & Public Life :

  • Began as a police constable, later entering politics. Served as AIADMK’s Chennai District Secretary (1972–1987), Government chief whip in the Legislative Council, and Chairman of Metro Water Supply & Tamil Nadu Fisheries Development Corporation
  • A close confidante of M.G. Ramachandran (MGR), who named his daughters. His political network helped launch his educational initiatives

🎓 Vision & Educational Legacy :

  • Founded Sathyabama Engineering College in 1988, later becoming a deemed university; then established St. Joseph’s College of Engineering (1994) and Jeppiaar Engineering College (August 15, 2001), among others
  • Built an educational empire under the Jeppiaar Educational Trust, including Panimalar, St. Mary’s, SRR Engineering, dental and management institutes.
  • He was president of the Consortium of Professional, Arts & Science Colleges of Tamil Nadu and a key figure in making engineering education accessible via private institutions in TN

🏛 Commemoration & Succession :

  • Jeppiaar passed away on June 18, 2016, after experiencing breathlessness and cardiac congestion; his funeral was held at the Sathyabama University campus the next day
  • His legacy continues through a memorial museum inaugurated in June 2017 at Sathyabama University—the first museum in India dedicated to an educationist—curated by his daughter, Dr. Regeena J. Murli
  • Dr. Regeena was actively involved in founding Jeppiaar Engineering College and currently serves as its Managing Director, continuing her father's vision of quality, disciplined, and industry-focused education

Placements

📊 Placement Statistics (2023–2025) :

  • Highest package (2023): ₹12.5 LPA
  • Average package: ₹6.2 LPA
  • Median package: ₹5.8 LPA
  • Companies visited: ~45
  • Students placed: ~140 with 150 offers ( Placement rate ~60% depending on batch strength)
  • Top recruiters included: Infosys, TCS, Capgemini, Accenture, Amazon, MRF

🛠️ Training & Placement Support :

The college’s Training & Placement (T&P) department organizes a comprehensive 35‑day training program that includes:

  • Aptitude training (10 days)
  • Soft‑skills (3 days)
  • Company‑specific modules (2 days)
  • Mock interviews and group discussions (3 days)
  • C and data structures/algorithms training (7 days)
  • Department‑specific technical modules (6 days) Also features air‑conditioned facilities for assessments, interviews, and pre‑placement briefings

JEC maintains regular industry collaborations with major service and core companies (Infosys, Cognizant, Wipro, L&T, Accenture, Ashok Leyland, MRF, etc.) and supports internships, live industry projects, and entrepreneurship programs


🎯 Tips If You’re Considering Joining

  1. Focus on CSE, IT, or high-demand branches where placement statistics are stronger.
  2. Engage fully in all pre-placement training modules and mock assessments.
  3. Leverage internship opportunities early—thesis/internship projects help build experience
  4. Use alumni network and entrepreneurship cells to access real-world roles and startup opportunities
  5. Consider off-campus prep, especially if aspiring for top-tier product/tech firms.
