Semantic HTML tags

Semantic HTML tags define the purpose and meaning of content within an HTML document, making it more understandable for both browsers and developers. They provide structure and context, improving accessibility and search engine optimization (SEO).Naukri.com

Article

Represents a self-contained piece of content that could be independently distributed or reused.

Section

Defines a thematic grouping of content within a document.

Aside

Contains content that is tangentially related to the main content, often used for sidebars.

Footer

Represents the footer of a section or document, typically containing information like copyright or contact details.

Time

Represents a specific time or date.
